About El-Motahida

Founded in 1980, El-Motahida Marble has become a top supplier of Egyptian and imported marble and granite. We’ve expanded globally with clients across Europe, the Gulf, and beyond.

Our reputation is built on four decades of craftsmanship, precision, and partnerships with leading construction firms like El-Mokawloon El-Arab.

Our Mission

To source, craft, and deliver the finest marble and granite with uncompromising precision—transforming raw stone into enduring art while exceeding client expectations at every step.

Our Vision

To be the world’s most trusted name in premium marble and granite, setting benchmarks for quality, innovation, and timeless design.
